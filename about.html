About Us

Who We Are

Anshumat Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals through knowledge, mentorship, and career development.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for underprivileged communities, spread awareness about social causes, and encourage youth to participate in meaningful initiatives.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has access to education, equality, and opportunities to grow and live with dignity.
